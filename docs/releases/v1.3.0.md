# Agentic Design System v1.3.0

This release closes the loop between design intent, rendered production evidence, and actionable
revision.

- Dogfoods decision provenance on the public `/trace` page, preserving verified
  decision → rule → constraint → evidence chains from a real prospective build.
- Makes missing `main` landmarks, loading/error live-region semantics, and CLS above `0.1`
  blocking rendered failures.
- Prevents missing semantic or CLS measurements from silently passing rendered authority.
- Adds eight structured diagnostic categories beneath the four ADS rubric scores, with
  minor/major/blocker severity, exact rendered location, falsifiable observations, and evidence.
- Converts major and blocker findings into bounded revision instructions and preserves
  finding → revision → evidence traceability across iterations.
- Extends the clean-install and release gate across Claude Code, Codex, Cursor, OpenClaw, and
  Hermes with production-gate and structured-finding smokes.

The structured categories diagnose why a score failed. They do not replace the four-score verdict
layer or turn subjective judgments into deterministic gates.
